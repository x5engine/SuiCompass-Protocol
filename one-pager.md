# SuiCompass: AI-Native DeFi Operating System

**Project Name:** SuiCompass  
**Website:** [suicompass.com](https://suicompass.com)  
**Stage:** Mainnet Live (MVP Launched)  
**Launch / Ecosystem:** Sui Blockchain  

### Core Use Case
SuiCompass is the first AI-native DeFi operating system built on the Sui blockchain. We enable users to execute complex decentralized finance operations—including Real-World Asset (RWA) tokenization, smart liquid staking, and institutional-grade portfolio management—using simple natural language commands.

### Fit Summary
The barrier to entry in DeFi is too high, and traditional finance (TradFi) remains siloed. SuiCompass bridges this gap by replacing complex, intimidating UIs with an intuitive AI Copilot. By leveraging Sui's object-centric architecture for parallel execution and embedded security, we are uniquely positioned to capture both retail users seeking automated yield and institutional clients looking to tokenize real estate, invoices, and bonds efficiently.
